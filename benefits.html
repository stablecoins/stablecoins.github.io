Benefits of USDG

Discover why USDG is the superior choice for stablecoin users

Community Owned

Unlike USDC, USDG is a decentralized stablecoin owned by the DAO of token holders. This means you have a voice in governance and future development decisions. The community collectively decides on important protocol changes, fee structures, and treasury management.

Transparent Backing

While USDC is backed by tokenized government bonds, USDG maintains its peg through a diversified and transparent reserve system. All reserves are on-chain and can be verified by anyone at any time, ensuring complete transparency and trust in the stability of USDG.

Competitive Yield

USDC offers 0% yield, but USDG provides approximately 3% yield as a STABLE token. This means your assets continue to grow while maintaining stability. The yield is generated through a combination of treasury management, protocol fees, and DeFi strategies, all governed by USDG holders.

Enhanced Security

USDG implements advanced security measures and regular audits to ensure the safety of your assets across all supported networks. The smart contracts have been thoroughly audited by leading security firms, and the protocol includes safety mechanisms to protect against potential vulnerabilities.
